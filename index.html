About Us

Welcome to The Rook's Chess Academy

Our Mission

To make chess accessible, enjoyable, and rewarding for everyone. We believe chess enhances critical thinking, problem-solving skills, and strategic awareness, helping players of all levels grow, learn, and succeed.

Our Vision

To create a global community of chess enthusiasts who are dedicated to mastering the game, competing at the highest levels, and inspiring future generations through the power of chess.

Our Values

We value inclusivity, passion, dedication, and continuous improvement. We aim to foster an environment where every player, regardless of experience, feels motivated to learn, compete, and achieve their best.

Our Services

Our services are designed to provide top-notch educational experiences, tailored to your unique learning needs.

Online One-to-One Classes

Personalized learning experiences in a virtual setting with expert instructors.

Online Group Classes

Interactive learning in a collaborative online environment with peers.

Private Home Tuition

One-on-one sessions in the comfort of your home for a more focused approach.
